XY heatmap quantile chart

A good way of showing the patterns between 2 categories of data, less good at showing fine differences in amounts

Some useful settings in the index.js

  • rotateLabels – set to 'true' to rotate column labels 45 degrees to prevent overlapping
  • showValues – set to 'true' to display values of each block
  • scaleBreaks – define the ranges of your colour scale
  • scaleType – choose from 'sequentialRed' or 'sequentialBlue' to colour your chart
